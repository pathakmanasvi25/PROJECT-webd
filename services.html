Circulation Services

Library materials are borrowed and returned at the circulation desk, which is located near the main entrance. Reserved books, audio visual material, periodicals, reference books and CDs / DVDs can be used with in library.

Photocopying / Printing Service

Photocopying and printing facility is available with the library. Users can get the photocopy of required information from reference materials (like reference books, encyclopedias, dictionaries, journals and magazines etc.) on payment.

Reference Services

Library provides reference and referral service to its users. Queries are answered using all possible sources. If the required information source is not available / accessible at The Cosmic Athenaeum, the users are referred to other libraries where the required information may be available.

Access to computer

We provide access to computer and internet on a slot division basis. One would have to register first and should be an exclusive member of the library. Students with a valid id proof can use our computers at 50% discount for all their projects, assignments and such.

Quiet study spaces

Facing lots of disturbance at home while working on a project or studying for finals? We have got you. Our library has dedicated ample space for our regular scholars and even new ones to sit back,relax and focus with all the information they need at their disposal!

e-Books

Slowly but steadily, we are digitalizing all our books so that our members can have access to everything through a more convenient manner. We currently boast of a collection of 1000+ e-books, e-journals, e-essays and e-projects at our services which are available to our members.
